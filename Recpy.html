FAQ

How do I add a recipe?

To add a recipe, simply navigate to the 'Add' page and fill in the required details. It's quick and easy!

Can I edit my recipes later?

Yes, you can edit your recipes at any time by visiting the 'Edit' page and making the desired changes. It's hassle-free!

How do I delete a recipe?

Deleting a recipe is simple. Just go to the 'Edit' page, select the recipe, and hit the delete button. It's that easy!

Is there a limit to the number of recipes I can add?

No, there's no limit! You can add as many recipes as you like and keep building your culinary collection.

Can I share my recipes with others?

Absolutely! Tasty is all about sharing the love for food. You can easily share your recipes with friends and fellow foodies.
